Knowledge Tree

the roots.

every civilisation starts by asking questions. books stories and shared knowledge became the roots that support every new discovery. without these foundations the tree could never begin to grow.

the trunk.

history carries knowledge from one generation to another. every success and every mistake helps strengthen the trunk and supports everything that grows above it.

the branches.

knowledge spreads into science maths engineering medicine and art. each branch grows in a different direction but they all remain connected to the same foundation.

the leaves.

every leaf represents a new idea. together they allow humanity to keep learning and help the tree continue growing into the future.

the tree never stops growing.
